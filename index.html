OUR STORY

At AATHMA Counselling, we don't just care for your mental health we redefine how you access it. With Dr.CAFFE we have created a global virtual wellness hub that makes connecting with top mental health professionals as easy as grabbing a cup of coffee. Imagine a space where the best therapists, counselors and mental health experts from across the globe are just a click away. At Dr.CAFFE the world’s finest mental health professionals are ready to help you thrive.Dr.Jaseel was not just an ordinary doctor he was a visionary, a trailblazer with a mission to bring change to the world. As a compassionate physician he had spent years treating patients who despite their physical ailments often battled silent storms within their minds. The endless lines of people seeking solace and the stigma surrounding mental health deeply moved him. He realized the world needed more than just conventional solutions it needed innovation, accessibility, and a personal touch. One evening, over steaming cups of chai in his hometown.Dr. Jaseel and his childhood friend sat under the stars discussing life’s challenges. His friend a tech genius with a heart of gold shared how people in his life struggled with mental health issues yet found it impossible to seek help. Why isn’t there a tool a companion for everyone, everywhere at every moment of need? his friend pondered aloud. That was the spark. Inspired by this idea, Dr. Jaseel envisioned creating something groundbreaking a platform that would make mental health support as accessible as a heartbeat. They wanted to build more than just an app they dreamed of creating a movement.It wasn’t just about therapists or self-help tips, it was about offering a safe space for every individual regardless of their age, location, or circumstances. Dr. Jaseel and his friend poured their hearts into developing AATHMA, a mental health app designed to be there for people during life’s every moment big or small. The app wasn’t just about addressing crises but also about celebrating victories, navigating uncertainties, and fostering growth. They incorporated features like AI-driven emotional support, live therapy sessions, guided meditations, and personalized well-being plans. Their approach was revolutionary. AATHMA wasn’t just an app it was a companion. It listened when no one else could. It guided users through sleepless nights, anxious days, and overwhelming situations. The team even added an anonymous community forum where people could share, connect, and heal together. As AATHMA launched, it wasn’t just another app in the digital world it became a lifeline for millions. Stories began pouring in a young student overcoming exam stress, a new mother navigating postpartum depression, an entrepreneur rediscovering balance. The impact was monumental and Dr. Jaseel’s small-town dream grew into a global phenomenon. What made AATHMA truly exceptional was its core belief Mental health isn’t a luxury it’s a necessity. Dr. Jaseel and his friend understood that life’s challenges don’t wait so why should support? They ensured that AATHMA was available 24/7 bridging the gap between need and help with no barriers of time, cost, or stigma. Today, Dr. Jaseel is celebrated not just as a doctor but as a pioneer who dared to address what the world often ignored. AATHMA stands as a testament to his unwavering belief that everyone everywhere deserves mental health support in every moment of life. And as he likes to say, The mind is the key to life’s greatest victories. Let’s keep it healthy always.
